ABOUT ME

Hi I'm Smitha Subhram!

I’m a person who believes in mental connections. I try to form an intimate connection with each of my clients so as to delve deeper into the root of their problems. Starting my career as a University Lecturer, I simultaneously set out on my journey towards healing by practicing Mindfulness thereby completing my training as an NLP Coach. Eventually, acquiring the certification as an internationally licensed Heal Your Life facilitator.

ONE ON ONE COACHING

One-on-one coaching is the process of one person assisting another in resolving a problem or enhancing performance. With this, I can act as your navigator, showing you the way around stumbling blocks and saving you the trouble of having to figure things out on your own.

GROUP COACHING

The purpose of group coaching is to develop self-discovery among a group of people with similar interests, leading to action-based plans that will break down barriers between individuals so they may share ideas, information, excitement, and wisdom.
